About Mobile Translate

Mobile Translate is a specialized tool designed to streamline the localization process for mobile applications. Our platform automatically generates properly structured localization files for both Android and iOS platforms, ensuring your app speaks your users' language perfectly.

Android Support

Automatically generates the values/strings.xml structure with proper language-specific folders (values-ar, values-fr, etc.), ready to be integrated into your Android project.

iOS Support

Creates .lproj directories with Localizable.strings files, maintaining the exact structure required by Xcode for seamless integration.

Multiple Languages

Support for numerous languages and regional variants, ensuring your app reaches a global audience with accurate translations.

Easy Integration

Download your localization files in a ZIP format, ready to be dropped into your mobile project with no additional configuration needed.

How To Use

1. Select your target platform (Android/iOS)
2. Choose your desired languages for translation
3. Click the submit translation request and wait until the localization process finishes
4. Review the translated strings in their different locales 5. Extract the ZIP file and add the contents to your mobile project
